Effective Date: 2026-04-14 14:09:53 UTC

At Software4U, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the emails collected from users who sign up early for our services.

When you sign up early, we collect your email address to communicate important information regarding:
1. **Early Signup Benefits**: Your email will be used to inform you about exclusive benefits and offers available to early signups.
2. **Launch Announcements**: We will use your email to send you announcements related to our product launch, keeping you updated on our progress.
3. **Optional Mailing List Signup**: After our launch, you will have the option to sign up for our mailing list to receive updates, newsletters, and additional content related to our services.

## Use of Your Information
Your email address will only be used for the purposes stated above and will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

## Your Rights
You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, as well as the right to request that we correct or delete your data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
